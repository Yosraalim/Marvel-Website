THE MCU HISTORY

 

 

The Walt Disney Studios division of the Walt Disney Company owns Marvel Studios, LLC, which was initially known as Marvel Films from 1993 to 1996. It is the highest-grossing film franchise of all time, having grossed over $28 billion at the global box office. 

 

But originally …..

 

It was in 1939 when Marvel was started as Timely Comics by Martin Goodman, and then it was commonly known as Atlas Comics by 1951. Later on, by 1961 the Marvel era began. It was the year that the company had launched The Fantastic Four and other superhero titles created by the infamous Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, Steve Ditko and many others.

 

Marvel Studios' brand new president Kevin Feige was promoted to President of Production in 2007, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe was born. Since then, Marvel Studios has created 30 films and 9 MCU series, and at least 12 more are currently in development. In the following years, Feige's status within Marvel grew, and he was promoted into more roles as the MCU became more successful.
