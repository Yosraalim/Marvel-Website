MCU Actors

 

 

In the history of Marvel comic books, the company has had reams of characters - somewhere around 8000 staples and potentially more if you count one-offs, ancillary characters, and multiverse versions.

but the question is......

How many actors has Marvel Studios actually hired since it's 2007?

As of right now, Marvel Studios uses Sarah Finn Casting for all of its casting needs. Occasionally, Sarah Finn Casting contracts with an outside casting company to assist in certain regional markets. Although Marvel Studios has never officially reported their overall number of hired actors, it has been speculated that since 2007 marvel has hired well over 2500 casts members, with many ranging from star actors, to side actors, to background actors and many more. And it has been reported that this number will only further increase with the new upcoming 'Phases 5 and 6' in the current productions.

It's considered by many fans that Robert Downey Jr. is the godfather of the Marvel Cinematic Universe since he had the first solo film with Iron Man and has appeared in 10 MCU films since.
